Thank You For Visiting Burgundy for Life!

Burgundy for Life Shop is an online shop hosted by the AVM Awareness Project, which strives to spread Arteriovenous Malformation and Aneurysm Awareness through hand-designed and hand-made gifts for survivors and their supporters. They are designed or created by an AVM Survivor or supporter. Burgundy is the color of AVM and Brain Aneurysm Awareness Ribbon, and Life is for our goal of reaching out to people and spreading awareness with the hope of making positive differences in people's lives.
See what causes and organizations we support in our Causes & Donations page. The shop is run by an AVM Survivor in NYC, and most products were designed and produced by her.
